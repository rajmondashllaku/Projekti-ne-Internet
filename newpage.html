Top Rated TV Shows

Top 10 as rated by our critics

  1. Band Of Brothers (2001)

    The story of Easy Company of the U.S. Army 101st Airborne Division, and their mission in World War II Europe, from Operation Overlord, through V-J Day.

  2. Breaking Bad (2008)

    A high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer turns to manufacturing and selling meth in order to secure his family's future.

  3. Chernobyl (2019)

    In April 1986, an explosion at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics becomes one of the world's worst man-made catastrophes.

  4. The Wire (2002)

    The Baltimore drug scene, as seen through the eyes of drug dealers and law enforcement.

  5. Avatar:The Last Airbender(2005)

    In a war-torn world of elemental magic, a young boy reawakens to undertake a dangerous mystic quest to fulfill his destiny as the Avatar, and bring peace to the world.

  6. Sherlock (2010)

    A modern update finds the famous sleuth and his doctor partner solving crime in 21st century London.

  7. When They See Us (2019)

    Five teens from Harlem become trapped in a nightmare when they're falsely accused of a brutal attack in Central Park. Based on the true story.

  8. Black Mirror (2011)

    An anthology series exploring a twisted, high-tech multiverse where humanity's greatest innovations and darkest instincts collide.

  9. The Office (2005)

    A mockumentary on a group of typical office workers, where the workday consists of ego clashes, inappropriate behavior, and tedium.

  10. Freaks And Geeks (1999)

    A high school mathlete starts hanging out with a group of burnouts while her younger brother navigates his freshman year.
